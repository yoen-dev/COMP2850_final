Get started in minutes

How it works

1
Create your account & set up your profile

Sign up as a subscriber or health professional. Enter your height, weight, and daily calorie target — our onboarding guide walks you through everything in under 2 minutes.

2
Log your meals and exercise daily

Use the food diary to record what you eat across breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks. Log workouts to see your calories in vs. out. Your nutrition donut updates with every entry.

3
Discover recipes and plan ahead

Browse the recipe library, save favourites, and use the meal planner to build a day that fits your calorie budget. Compare dishes side by side to make smarter choices.

4
Get support from a health professional

Your assigned professional monitors your progress, sends personalised advice through the messaging system, and adjusts your meal and training plan as you improve.
