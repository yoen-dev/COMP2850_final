Professional Help Centre

Pro Q&A

Everything health professionals need to know about managing clients, using the dashboard, and getting the most from NourishWell Pro.

🔍
Client Management
How do I view a client's dashboard? +

Click on any client's name or avatar in the left sidebar. This loads their full profile on the right, including their analytics, messages, meal plan, and appointments. You can also click any row in the Client Snapshot table on the overview dashboard.

How do I search for a specific client? +

Use the Search clients... input at the top of the client sidebar. The list filters in real time as you type a client's name.

How do I update a client's status? +

Click the status badge (e.g. "Low protein", "On track", "Missed 3 days") next to a client's name in the sidebar. Each click cycles through the three status options: OK (green), Warning (amber), and Urgent (red).

What does the overall health score mean? +

The health score is a composite metric out of 100, made up of four categories:

  • Nutrition Quality — How well the client is hitting their macro targets
  • Log Consistency — How regularly they log their meals
  • Hydration — Estimated from logged drinks
  • Exercise — Workouts completed vs. weekly target

Click any category row to expand a brief explanation. These scores are calculated from the client's backend data once integrated.

Analytics
What does the calorie trend chart show? +

The calorie trend line chart shows the client's daily calorie intake over the past 30 days. The horizontal dashed line represents their daily calorie target. Gaps or drops can indicate missed logging days or low intake periods worth addressing.

How is BMI calculated? +

BMI is calculated using the standard formula: weight (kg) ÷ height (m)². The colour-coded gradient bar shows their BMI category:

  • Under 18.5 — Underweight
  • 18.5–24.9 — Healthy
  • 25–29.9 — Overweight
  • 30+ — Obese

BMI is updated automatically when the client updates their weight in their profile.

What do the macro bars show? +

The macro bars compare the client's actual intake vs. their target for carbohydrates, protein, fat, and sugar. The filled portion shows actual; the full bar represents target. A short bar means they're under their target, which is often most critical for protein.

Messaging
How do I send a message to a client? +

Select the client from the sidebar, then click the 💬 Messages tab. Type your message in the input box at the bottom and press Send → or hit Enter. You can also use the quick-tag buttons (e.g. "Great progress", "Protein reminder") to fill in common messages instantly.

How do I know if a client has sent me a message? +

A red dot appears on the envelope icon (✉️) in the top navigation bar when you have unread messages. Click the icon to open the messages panel, which shows all clients with recent messages. Unread conversations are highlighted. Clicking a client in the panel jumps directly to their chat tab.

What are the quick-tag buttons? +

Quick tags are pre-written message templates that fill the message box with one click. Available tags include:

  • ✓ Great progress — Positive reinforcement
  • 🥩 Protein reminder — Nudge to hit protein targets
  • 📅 Check-in reminder — Appointment reminder
  • 📋 Plan updated — Notify client of plan changes

You can still edit the message before sending.

Plans & Appointments
How do I edit a client's meal plan? +

Select the client and click the 📋 Fitness Plan tab. Click Edit Plan on the Weekly Meal Plan card. An inline editor appears with one input per day of the week. Update the meals as needed and click Save Plan. Changes are reflected immediately in the client's plan view.

How do I edit a client's training schedule? +

On the Fitness Plan tab, click Edit Plan on the Training Schedule card. Update each day's training entry and click Save Plan. The schedule updates in real time.

How do I book a new appointment? +

On the 📅 Appointments tab, click + Book New Appointment. A modal will appear — select the date, time, and appointment type (Check-in, Plan Review, Initial Assessment, etc.) then click Confirm Appointment. The appointment appears at the top of the list with a Pending status.

How do I confirm or cancel an appointment? +

Click any appointment card to toggle its status between Pending and Confirmed. To delete an appointment, click the ✕ button on the right of the card.

How do I add pro notes for a client? +

In the Fitness Plan tab, find the Pro Notes section. Click Edit to open a text editor. Write your notes (observations, recommendations, reminders) and click Save. Notes are saved per client and visible only to you.

Account & Settings
How do I update my professional profile? +

Click your avatar (DR) in the top right → Personal Details. You can update your name, email, licence number, specialisation, years of experience, and maximum client capacity. Click Update Profile to save.

How do I set client alert thresholds? +

Go to avatar menu → Preferences → Client Alerts. Use the sliders to set:

  • Calorie threshold — Flag clients whose intake falls below this percentage of their target
  • Missed log days — Alert you when a client hasn't logged for this many days
How do I switch to my personal user view? +

Click Switch to User View in the top navigation bar. This takes you to your own personal NourishWell dashboard where you can log your own meals, track your nutrition, and use all subscriber features. Click Switch to Pro to return to the professional dashboard.

How do I log out? +

Click your avatar (DR) in the top right corner → Log Out. You will be redirected to the NourishWell login page.
