How can we help you?

Everything you need to get the most out of NourishWell — from logging meals to understanding your nutrition data.

Getting Started
How do I set up my profile for the first time? +

When you first log in, NourishWell will show you an onboarding guide with 3 simple steps:

  • Step 1 — Enter your name, email and date of birth
  • Step 2 — Add your height, weight and activity level. We automatically calculate your daily calorie target using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula
  • Step 3 — Choose your goal (weight loss, muscle gain, maintain, or general health)

You can update any of this at any time from Personal Details in your avatar menu.

What is my daily calorie target based on? +

Your daily calorie target is calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, which takes into account your height, weight, age, and activity level to estimate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).

You can view and adjust your target at any time in Settings → Personal Details → Health Profile. If your goal changes, update your target accordingly.

Can I switch between subscriber and professional mode? +

If your account has professional access, you can switch between views using the Switch to User View button in the top navigation bar of the pro dashboard. This shows your own personal health dashboard.

To return to the professional dashboard, click Switch to Pro which appears in the top nav when you're in user view.

Food Diary
How do I log a meal? +

There are two ways to log food:

  • Click Record Meal on the Record Today page to open the food panel
  • Click + Add item under any meal column in the daily log

In the food panel, select your meal slot (Breakfast, Morning Snack, Lunch, etc.), search or browse the food list, and tap + to add items. When done, press Done to save to your diary.

How do I delete a food entry? +

In the meal log section, each food item has a small ✕ button on the right side. Click it to remove that entry. Your daily calorie total will update automatically.

How do I log exercise? +

Click the Exercise button on the Record Today page. Search for your activity (running, swimming, yoga, etc.), enter the duration, and press + to add it. Your exercise activity chart updates to show your weekly pattern.

Why is my calorie count an estimate? +

The macro breakdown (carbs, protein, fat, sugar) shown in the donut chart is currently estimated based on typical macro ratios for your logged calories. For precise macros, you'll need to log foods with specific nutritional data — this is a feature we're working to improve with backend integration.

Recipes
How do I save a recipe to my favourites? +

On the Recipe page, each recipe card has a heart icon (🤍) in the bottom right. Click it to save — it will turn red (❤️). To remove a recipe from favourites, click the heart again or use the Remove button on the Favourites page.

How do I search for recipes? +

Use the search bar at the top of the Recipe page to search by recipe name, ingredient, or tag (e.g. "vegan", "quick"). You can also use the filter tags below the search bar to browse by category: Healthy, Quick, Vegan, High Protein, or My Recipes.

Can I add my own recipes? +

Yes! Click the + Add Recipe button on the Recipe page. Fill in the name, emoji, calories, prep time, category, ingredients (one per line), and cooking steps. Your recipe will be saved and appear under the My Recipes filter. Custom recipes persist in your browser's local storage.

How do I rate and review a recipe? +

Click the star rating on any recipe card to open the rate & review panel. Select 1–5 stars and optionally write a text review. Click Save Review to save. Your rating will be reflected on the card immediately and persists across sessions.

What is Recipe Compare? +

Recipe Compare lets you select up to 4 recipes and view their nutritional data side by side in a grouped bar chart. It shows calories, protein, carbs, and fat for each recipe. Use it to make smarter meal choices based on your goals. Access it from the Recipe Compare section in the sidebar.

Nutrition & Goals
What do the colours on the donut chart mean? +

The donut chart on your Record Today page shows your macro breakdown:

  • Teal — Carbohydrates
  • Amber — Protein
  • Blue — Fat
  • Pink — Sugar

The percentage complete shown in the centre is your progress towards your daily calorie target.

How do I change my macro targets? +

Go to your avatar menu → Preferences → Nutrition Goals. Use the sliders to adjust your target percentages for carbohydrates, protein, and fat. The sliders are linked so you can see the balance between macros as you adjust.

How does the Meal Planner work? +

The Meal Planner helps you plan your day's meals within your calorie budget. Here's how to use it:

  • Set a preference filter (Quick, Low-carb, High-protein, etc.)
  • The planner suggests recipes for Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
  • Click 🔒 Lock to fix a meal you like
  • Click 🔄 to swap an individual recipe
  • Click Refresh all to regenerate the whole meal slot
  • When happy, click Confirm Plan →
Account & Settings
How do I update my personal details? +

Click your avatar (initials) in the top right → Personal Details. Here you can update your name, email, date of birth, height, weight, activity level, and daily calorie target. Click Save Changes or Update Profile to apply.

How do I change my password? +

Go to avatar menu → Personal Details → Change Password. Enter your current password, then your new password (minimum 8 characters), and confirm it. Click Update Password.

How do I manage notification preferences? +

Go to avatar menu → Preferences → Notifications. You can toggle on or off:

  • Meal reminders
  • Weekly nutrition summary
  • Recipe recommendations
  • Messages from your health professional
How do I read messages from my health professional? +

Click the envelope icon (✉️) in the top navigation bar. A panel will slide open showing your conversation with Dr. Rivera. Unread messages are indicated by a red dot on the icon. You can reply directly from the panel.

How do I log out? +

Click your avatar (initials) in the top right corner → Log Out. You will be redirected to the NourishWell login page.
